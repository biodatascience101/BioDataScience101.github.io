Biodatascience101 is a Sino-Danish Center sponsored project presenting a series of lectures on biological data-science modelling, visualization and machine-learning in Python. The emphasis of these materials is not just to introduce users to practical biological data-science techniques, but also demonstrate scientific analysis of results through the lens of academic and biotechnological research.

Our next event is our free Copenhagen pilot even at the Danish Technical University of Denmark on March 24th 2020.

This work was supported in part by the Sino-Danish Center for Education and Research (SDC), Novozymes and the Novo Nordisk Foundation.
